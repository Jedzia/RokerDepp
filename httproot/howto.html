Vu+ Streaming Proxy

Gratulations, you installed vustreamproxy. A middle man, who converts RSS-based media streams. Currently only the conversion from ogg-vorbis to mp3 format ist supported. More formats may follow later.

You can get the source at the github repository: https://github.com/Jedzia/vustreamproxy
